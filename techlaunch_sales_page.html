Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need prior experience to join?
No. Starter-tier packages begin from scratch — we assume nothing. Pro and Premium tiers require basic familiarity with computers and ideally some exposure to programming, but a strong willingness to learn is the only real requirement.
Can I take the tracks at the same time?
For bundles, we recommend running them sequentially — AI first, then Automation, then App/Web3 — because each builds on the last. However, motivated students can discuss overlapping schedules with their instructor.
What if I miss a live session?
All live sessions are recorded and shared within 24 hours. You can watch the replay, then post your questions in the class group or raise them at the next session.
Is the certificate recognised by employers?
Our certificates are issued on completion of projects and assessments — not just attendance. Employers care far more about your portfolio projects, which are designed to demonstrate real skills. We coach you on how to present them effectively.
What equipment do I need?
A laptop or desktop computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux), a stable internet connection, and a working camera and microphone for live sessions. A smartphone alone is not sufficient for coding tracks.
What is the refund policy?
We offer a full refund within 7 days of the first class if you decide the programme isn't right for you. After that, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. We'd rather work with you to find a solution than lose you as a student.
